# 📊 Sales Performance Dashboard

## 📌 Project Overview

This project presents an interactive Sales Performance Dashboard designed to analyze business performance across multiple dimensions including time, product categories, and geographic regions.

The goal of this analysis is to move beyond tracking sales and focus on understanding profitability and business efficiency.

---

## 🎯 Business Problem

Many businesses focus on increasing sales without fully understanding whether those sales are generating real profit.

This project answers a key question:

👉 Are we growing profitably, or just increasing revenue?

---

## 🔍 Key Insights

* The business achieved strong growth and the highest profit in the most recent year.
* Performance was not consistent, with a noticeable decline in 2021 followed by recovery.
* Some product categories generate high sales but relatively low profit margins.
* High-margin categories (e.g., Mobiles) represent strong opportunities for profitability improvement.

---

## 📈 Dashboard Features

* KPI tracking (Total Sales, Net Sales, Profit, Margin, Growth)
* Sales trend analysis over time
* Category performance comparison
* Country-level performance analysis
* Interactive filtering (Year, Category, Country)

---

## 💡 Business Recommendations

* Focus on high-margin product categories to improve profitability
* Optimize pricing and discount strategies
* Shift business focus from revenue growth to profit maximization
